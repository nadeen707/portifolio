Achievements
Shadow teacher

The task of being a shadow teacher requires important values such as understanding each student in need and how they can be benefited. It also requires persistence and yet a delicate personality that the student would seek comfort with. As a shadow teacher, I visit students 4 times a week to make sure that they aren't struggling during that lesson. And if they were, I would sit and explain it to them. I'm usually designated in the mathematics section as it is one of my strong suits, as well as the English section. This lasted 2 years, and I hope to continue it.

Teachers Assistant

twice a week I would help the head of the English department with grading students' quizzes and homeworks, and put them into the grade book. I would organize all the works each student has done by putting them in a folder specifically for their grade. I did this for all 11 and 12th graders. And since the students' grades are confidential, only the teacher and I have access to it, meaning there needs to be a high level of trust for both the teacher and the students.

Charity Leader

I gather anyone willing to help and we assist in cooking and packaging the foods. After that, we pass out the food to anyone in need. This helps us on so many levels and not just the gratitude we should already feel.
